## HR Questions

**Q: How many annual leave days do full-time employees receive?**
A: Full-time employees receive 20 working days for 0-2 completed years of service, 23 working days for 3-5 years, and 25 working days for 6+ years. See HR Policy Manual §5.

**Q: How many sick days are available per year?**
A: Nexus provides up to 10 paid sick days per year, separate from annual leave. See HR Policy Manual §6.

**Q: What is the probationary period for new permanent employees?**
A: New permanent employees serve a probationary period of up to three months, with structured 30-, 60-, and 90-day check-ins. See HR Policy Manual §3.3.

**Q: Where are leave requests submitted?**
A: Leave requests are submitted in Workday and approved by the reporting manager. See HR Policy Manual §5.3.

## IT Questions

**Q: What is the minimum password length?**
A: Passwords must be at least 14 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character requirements. See IT Policy §3.1.

**Q: Is MFA required?**
A: Yes. MFA is mandatory for NexusMail, NexusConnect, NexusDocs, NexusBoard, NexusFlow, NexusHR Portal, VPN, and production/cloud environments. See IT Policy §4.

**Q: When is VPN required?**
A: Employees must use NexusSecure VPN when connecting to internal Nexus systems from outside an office location. Public Wi-Fi requires VPN before accessing any Nexus system. See IT Policy §5.

**Q: Can employees install any software they want?**
A: No. Unauthorized software requires approval from IT & Internal Systems, and many tools are requested through NexusBoard. See IT Policy §6.

## Security Questions

**Q: What is the default classification for a new document with no explicit label?**
A: Tier 3 — Confidential. See Information Security Policy §2.

**Q: Can customer data be entered into public AI tools?**
A: No. Customer data must never be entered into unapproved AI tools. See Information Security Policy §3 and IT Policy §7.

**Q: Are personal USB drives allowed?**
A: Personal or unknown-origin USB drives are prohibited by default on Nexus devices. See Information Security Policy §8.

**Q: What should an employee do after clicking a suspected phishing link?**
A: Disconnect from the network, report to IT Security via the emergency hotline or NexusConnect #security-incident channel, and change affected passwords using NexusVault. See Information Security Policy §6.

## Finance Questions

**Q: When are itemized receipts required?**
A: Itemized receipts are required for any expense over $25 USD. See Finance & Expense Policy §2.3.

**Q: How soon must expenses be submitted?**
A: Expenses must be submitted in Workday within 30 calendar days. Expenses over 90 days old require Director approval and may be denied. See Finance & Expense Policy §2.4.

**Q: How long does reimbursement take after approval?**
A: Approved expense reports are disbursed by direct deposit within 5-7 business days of final approval. See Finance & Expense Policy §2.5.

**Q: Who approves a purchase request above $10,000?**
A: Purchases above $10,000 require CFO involvement plus the relevant C-Suite sponsor, consistent with the Company Profile and Finance Policy approval thresholds. See Finance & Expense Policy §6.2 and Company Profile §9.3.

## Legal Questions

**Q: When is an NDA required?**
A: An approved NDA is required before confidential information is shared in sales, partnership, vendor, proof-of-concept, or other external engagements involving non-public information. See Legal & Compliance Manual §2.1.

**Q: Who owns work created by employees within the scope of employment?**
A: Nexus owns employee work product created within the scope of employment. See Legal & Compliance Manual §3.1.

**Q: Can employees sign contracts on behalf of Nexus?**
A: Only authorized signatories may execute contracts. Approval authority does not equal signature authority. See Legal & Compliance Manual §5.1.

## General Policy Questions

**Q: Which days are Core Collaboration Days for hybrid employees?**
A: Tuesday through Thursday are the standard Core Collaboration Days. See General Company Policies §2 and Company Profile §11.

**Q: Which tool should be used for official external business correspondence?**
A: NexusMail is the official channel for formal or external communications. See General Company Policies §3 and IT Policy §8.

**Q: Can employees rely on AI-generated output as final work product?**
A: No. AI-generated output must receive human review, especially for code, legal, financial, or customer-facing work. See General Company Policies §7.
