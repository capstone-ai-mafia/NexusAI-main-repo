## Core Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Nexus | Nexus Technologies Inc., the company described in the Company Profile. |
| Nexons | Informal internal term for Nexus employees, contractors, and temporary staff. |
| ELT | Executive Leadership Team, meaning the C-Suite collectively. |
| DRI | Directly Responsible Individual; the named person accountable for a deliverable or decision. |
| Squad / Pod | A cross-functional team assembled around a product initiative, customer segment, or business priority. |
| Core Collaboration Days | Tuesday through Thursday, the default in-office days for hybrid employees. |
| Core Hours | 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time, when employees are expected to be reachable. |
| L1-L7 | Company-wide leveling framework used for roles, promotion, compensation, and some travel/card rules. |
| HRBP | HR Business Partner. |
| CHRO | Chief Human Resources Officer. |
| CFO | Chief Financial Officer. |
| CTO | Chief Technology Officer. |
| CISO | Chief Information Security Officer. |
| General Counsel | Leader of Legal & Compliance. |
| DPO | Data Protection Officer. |

## Nexus Systems

| System | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Workday | System of record for HR, expenses, procurement requests, vendor data, PTO, and related workflows. |
| NexusFlow | Nexus flagship workflow automation and integration platform. |
| NexusDocs | Internal documentation, wiki, and secure document-sharing platform. |
| NexusConnect | Internal chat and collaboration tool. |
| NexusMail | Approved email system for official correspondence. |
| NexusMeet | Video meeting platform. |
| NexusBoard | IT/software/project request portal used for IT and software requests. |
| NexusVault | Company-approved password manager. |
| NexusAuth | Company-approved MFA mobile app. |
| NexusSecure VPN | Corporate VPN required for remote access to internal systems. |
| Nexus Device Management (NDM) | Device management platform enforcing encryption, patches, lock/wipe, and compliance controls. |
| NexusTravel Portal | Travel booking platform for business travel. |
| NexusHub | Internal employee portal for company resources and updates. |

## Data Classification

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Public | Approved for external release. |
| Tier 2 — Internal | Non-sensitive internal information. |
| Tier 3 — Confidential | Sensitive business information such as financial forecasts, contracts, source code, employee records, and product roadmaps. |
| Tier 4 — Restricted | Highly sensitive information such as customer PII, payment data, authentication credentials, and encryption keys. |
